Abrian's Outbreak Presskit

Abrian's Outbreak is an action stealth puzzle game based in a fantasy world filled with fairies, deamons, elfs, dwarfs and robotic guards that wont let our character escape from the complicated walls of the greatest prison in the realm.

- KEY FEATURES -

Talk to prisoners to extract valuable information.

Tempt the prisoners to make them fight to mislead the guards.

Steal objects when noone's watching.

Bribe them to make your way out!
